# JLPT N3 Grammar Rules
## Overview
The JLPT N3 is a Japanese language proficiency test that covers grammar, vocabulary, and kanji. The test is divided into four sections: reading comprehension, grammar, vocabulary, and kanji.

## Grammar Rules
The JLPT N3 covers 57 essential grammar patterns, including:

*   Basic sentence structures
*   Verb conjugations
*   Adjective and adverb forms
*   Sentence connectors and conjunctions
*   Passive voice and causative forms

## Vocabulary List
The JLPT N3 vocabulary list includes:

*   1,000 words and phrases
*   Nouns (including common and proper nouns)
*   Verbs (including action and state verbs)
*   Adjectives (including descriptive and evaluative adjectives)
*   Adverbs (including manner and time adverbs)

## Kanji List
The JLPT N3 kanji list includes:

*   370 characters
*   Basic strokes and radicals
*   Common kanji used in everyday language

## Study Tips
To pass the JLPT N3, it's essential to:

*   Practice regularly
*   Focus on grammar and vocabulary
*   Use flashcards and language learning apps
*   Read Japanese texts and watch Japanese media
